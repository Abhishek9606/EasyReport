Tired of the Old Way of Report Making?

Asking professors and showing them the report 100 times manually is exhausting. EasyReport aims at making the overall process significantly better by providing a central system defining the rules and managing submissions seamlessly.

Get started as a Student Get started as a Faculty

For Students

  • View all subjects demanding a report in one place.
  • See exact formatting rules defined by faculties.
  • Download official example templates.
  • Upload finished reports seamlessly.

For Faculty

  • Define clear rules and provide templates.
  • Manage group and individual assignments.
  • Set deadlines easily.
  • Review submissions and tag them for iteration or completion.
